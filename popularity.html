Blooket Popularity

Blooket was a released in 2019, however it wasn't until late 2020 that it began to gain popularity. In almost every month in the winter of 2020-2021 the Blooket website doubled in traffic. Many months however experienced much more growth than that. From October to November, Blooket's traffic multiplied by almost 10 times. The growth has caused Blooket's servers to be overwhelmed frequently. The servers would often slow and crash.
